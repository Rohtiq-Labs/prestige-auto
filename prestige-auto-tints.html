Film Technology

Engineered
to Endure.

Heat Rejection
+

XPEL PRIME XR Plus™ ceramic window film rejects up to 98% of infrared heat before it enters the cabin — without compromising optical clarity or signal transparency for modern vehicles.

IR REJECTION 98%
UV Protection
+

Blocks 99.9% of harmful UV-A and UV-B rays. Protects both your vehicle's interior surfaces and your skin from cumulative sun damage — year round in all weather conditions.

UV REJECTION 99.9%
Self-Healing Technology
+

XPEL ULTIMATE PLUS™ PPF features an elastomeric polymer top coat. Light surface swirls and scuffs vanish on their own through heat activation — from sunlight or warm water — restoring a flawless finish.

~70°F ACTIVATION TEMP
Optical Clarity
+

XPEL film achieves near-perfect optical clarity — invisible to the eye and without any orange-peel texture. Once installed, the film disappears into the paint. Protection you can't see.

99.5% LIGHT TRANSMISSION (CLEAR)
Hydrophobic Performance
+

XPEL Fusion Plus ceramic coating creates a nano-ceramic bond with contact angles exceeding 110°. Water, mud, and contaminants sheet off effortlessly — keeping your vehicle cleaner, longer.

110° CONTACT ANGLE
